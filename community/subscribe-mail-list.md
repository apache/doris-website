# Subscribe to the Apache Doris Mailing List

<!-- Knowledge type: Procedural steps -->
<!-- Applicable scenarios: New user onboarding / Joining the community / Following community decisions -->

The mailing list is the most widely recognized way to communicate in an Apache community. It carries day-to-day questions, technical discussions, and decision-making. Its asynchronous, broadcast nature is well suited to open-source communities distributed around the world. This article describes how to subscribe to the Apache Doris `dev` mailing list in 5 steps.

## Mailing List Address Reference

Subscription, discussion, and unsubscription use different email addresses. They map to the following uses:

| Purpose | Email address | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Subscribe | `dev-subscribe@doris.apache.org` | Any subject and body |
| Confirmation | `dev-help@doris.apache.org` | Automatic reply, requires a follow-up reply to confirm |
| Daily discussion | `dev@doris.apache.org` | Used for questions and discussion after subscription |
| Unsubscribe | `dev-unsubscribe@doris.apache.org` | Any subject and body |

## Subscription Steps

### 1. Send a Subscription Email

Open your email client, create a new message, and send an email to `dev-subscribe@doris.apache.org`. The subject and body can be anything.

![step1](/images/subscribe-mail-list-step1.png)

### 2. Receive the Confirmation Email from dev-help

After completing step 1, you will receive a confirmation email from `dev-help@doris.apache.org`.

> If you do not receive the email after a long wait, check whether it has been blocked or automatically routed into folders such as "Subscriptions," "Spam," or "Promotions."

![step2](/images/subscribe-mail-list-step2.png)

### 3. Reply to the Confirmation Email

For the email received in the previous step, choose one of the following two methods:

- **Method A**: Reply directly to that email.
- **Method B**: Create a new email and set the recipient to the "Reply-To" address shown in the previous email.

Either way, the subject and body can be anything.

![step3](/images/subscribe-mail-list-step3.png)

### 4. Receive the Welcome Email

After completing step 3, you will receive a welcome email titled **WELCOME to dev@doris.apache.org**. Your subscription is now complete, and community updates will be delivered to you through this mailing list.

![step4](/images/subscribe-mail-list-step4.png)

### 5. Start a Mail Discussion (Optional)

Once you have subscribed, send an email to `dev@doris.apache.org` to start a discussion. All subscribers will receive the email.

## Unsubscribe from the Mailing List

To unsubscribe, send an email to `dev-unsubscribe@doris.apache.org` with any subject and body, and follow the instructions in the returned email to confirm.
