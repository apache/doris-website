Apache Doris + MCP: The Real-Time Analytical Data Platform for the Agentic AI Era

velodb.io · VeloDB Engineering Team2025年8月11日
Apache Doris is built to meet the data challenges in the agentic AI era, delivering real-time analytics at scale. But to use Doris's power for AI agents, you need a bridge between them.
